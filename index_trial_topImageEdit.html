Emergency Chat

Useful in any situation where speech is impossible but communication is necessary.

Simple easy to use messaging app.

Introducing the app that lets you communicate with people nearby when your voice is weak.

Familiar interface

Similar design with messaging apps you use everyday. You will know your way around quickly.

Usage Help?

Start Emergency Chat and give the phone to a person you want to talk to. They write something and give you the phone back. You reply and give them the phone back ... and so on. No Internet data required.

Minimalistic Design

No bells and whistles. Straight to what you need.

Easy to personalize

Try with different alert messages, and font sizes.
